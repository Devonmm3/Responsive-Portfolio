About me

I grew up in Connecticut and studied literature and education at the University of CT, where I graduated with my bacholers in 2010. After college I moved to California, where I taught third grade at a Charter school and decided that teaching wasn't for me. I worked in hospitality, insurance and became a certified yoga teacher before moving back to the East Coast in 2016.

After moving to Northern Virginia, I decided that I wanted to be a part of the tech world. I love the fast pace of the industry and that there are always more things to learn and discover. I also feel very strongly that Tech needs more women in the industry, and I want to be a part of it. I am so happy to be a part of the George Washington Bootcamp and I can't wait to see what the future holds for me.

Some of my favorite things to do are yoga, cooking (specifically vegan food), work out at the gym, read, spend time with my dog and be outside.
